  • Architected the full systems stack: turn management, hex-grid logic, roguelike progression, and inventory puzzle systems
  • Wrote complex recursive algorithms powering the core puzzle mechanic
  • Designed and programmed enemy AI behavior systems
  • Developed custom editor tools to support content creation and team workflows
  • Built full UI/UX across all game screens and menus
  • Cross-platform iOS & Android builds including profiling and performance optimization
  • Leaderboard networking and metadata architecture using Unity Cloud Services
  • Cross-platform save systems
  • Led a team of 4 developers across the full production lifecycle
